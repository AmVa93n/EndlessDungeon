How to Play?

The game will keep generating new levels indefinitely. Your goal is to advance as deep into the dungeon as you can without dying.

Reach the golden door in order to advance to the next level

Avoid monsters

As you progress, you'll encounter more monsters, and they will deal greater damage. Each monster successfully avoided will score you points according to its strength. Monsters you took damage from will score no points!
